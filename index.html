About us

Anderson is an independent, Gothenburg-based, eyewear brand that was created in 2021 by childhood friends Jennifer and Albin Andersson.

Combining directional design with everyday function, the collections are inspired by iconic, classic silhouettes, handcrafted from premium Gothenburg acetate. Retail partners include Yrgo, Browns, Rinascente, Selfridges, and YME Universe. In 2021, the brand opened its flagship store in Gothenburg.

To get in contact with us, send us an email to our Customer Service.
